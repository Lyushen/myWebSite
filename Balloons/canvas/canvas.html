Drawing on Canvas

Overview

  • When drawing on the canvas, we specify the position of the thing we are drawing with x and y coordinates.
    • The top left corner of the canvas has the coordinates: x=0, y=0
    • The top right corner of the canvas has the coordinates: x=WIDTHOFCANVAS, y=0
    • The bottom left corner of the canvas has the coordinates: x=0, y=HEIGHTOFCANVAS
    • The bottom right corner of the canvas has the coordinates: x=WIDTHOFCANVAS, y=HEIGHTOFCANVAS
  • We draw squares and rectangles with the fillRect method, which takes 4 arguments:
    • X coordinate of top left corner of the rectangle
    • Y coordinate of top left corner of the rectangle
    • Width of the rectangle
    • Height of the rectangle
  • To draw more complex shapes (circles, triangles, etc.) we use paths. If you have ever worked with Vector Graphics, the concepts are very similar.
